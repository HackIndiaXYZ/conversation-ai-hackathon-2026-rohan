## Live Interaction (1:15 - 2:30)
- **Step 1**: Click the Mic button.
- **Speech**: "Hi Dr. Chen, I'm from Proxa Pharma. I'd love to discuss the new clinical data for our SGLT2 inhibitor."
- **Observe**: The transcript updates in real-time as I speak.
- **Response**: Dr. Chen should respond skeptically about the data.
- **Observe**: Notice the low latency from when I stop speaking to when she begins her response, and how her mouth movements sync with the voice.
